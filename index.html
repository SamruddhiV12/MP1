About Me

I’m a UIUC student passionate about crafting responsive and accessible web experiences. This project demonstrates how much can be done with pure HTML, SCSS, and JavaScript—no libraries.

Every effect on this page—from the sticky shrinking navbar to modals and the carousel—is coded manually for clarity and maintainability.

My broader interest lies in combining design thinking and technical execution to deliver intuitive, human-centered interfaces.
